Privacy Policy for WiFi Bubbles

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

1. Location Data and Wi-Fi Scanning

Our app, WiFi Bubbles, requires the Location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) to function. This is a strict technical requirement enforced by the Android operating system to allow apps to scan for nearby Wi-Fi networks and retrieve network information (such as SSID, BSSID, channel, and signal strength).

We respect your privacy: We do NOT track, collect, store, or transmit your physical location, GPS coordinates, or any of the scanned Wi-Fi data to external servers. All Wi-Fi scanning and data processing occur strictly locally on your device.

2. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

We utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and use user data, including Device IDs, IP addresses, and usage data, to serve personalized ads. By using this app, you consent to this data collection as governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage and User Data

We do not collect or store any personal data directly. The app does not require user registration or account creation.
